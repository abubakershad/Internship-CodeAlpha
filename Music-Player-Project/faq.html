FAQ's

What is the purpose of this music player?

This music player is designed to give users a smooth and enjoyable way to listen to their favorite tracks. It provides basic controls like play, pause, next, and previous, along with a clean and user-friendly interface.

Can I create my own playlist?

Yes, the player allows you to create and manage your own playlists. You can organize your favorite songs in one place and enjoy them whenever you like.

Does this music player work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! The website is fully responsive, which means it works seamlessly on mobiles, tablets, and desktops without any issues.

Can I listen to music offline?

At the moment, the player is mainly designed for online streaming. However, if you upload local audio files from your device, you can listen to them directly in the player without an internet connection.

Can I upload my own music files?

Yes, you can upload audio files from your computer or mobile and play them directly inside the music player.

Which file formats are supported?

The player supports popular formats like MP3, WAV, and OGG, ensuring that most of your music collection will work perfectly.

Is there a search option available?

Yes, you can use the built-in search bar to quickly find your favorite songs or artists within the music library.

Does the player have volume control?

Definitely! You can increase, decrease, or mute the volume based on your preference.

Are shuffle and repeat features available?

Yes, the player comes with shuffle mode to play songs randomly and repeat mode to loop your favorite tracks.

Can I see album art while playing music?

Yes, the player displays album artwork (if available in the file) to make your listening experience more engaging.

Does it save my last played song?

In most cases, the player can remember your last played track and resume from where you left off.

Can I control the player with keyboard shortcuts?

Yes, you can use basic keyboard shortcuts like the spacebar for play/pause and arrow keys for skipping songs, depending on the setup.

Is this music player free to use?

Yes, it’s completely free for users. You don’t need to pay or subscribe to enjoy its features.

Do I need to install anything to use it?

No installation is required. Simply open the website in your browser, and the player will be ready to use.

Is it safe to upload my personal music files?

Yes, your files stay on your device and are only played locally in the browser. They are not shared or uploaded to any server.

Can I share my playlist with friends?

Currently, playlists are personal. Future updates may include sharing features.

Does the music player support background play?

Yes, you can keep the music running in the background while browsing other pages or doing other work.

How do I report a bug or issue?

You can contact the developer through the “Support” or “Feedback” section on the website to report any bugs or improvements needed.

Can I use the music player without logging in?

Yes, most features are available without creating an account. However, logging in may unlock extra features like saving playlists.

Is there a dark mode or theme option?

Yes, the player includes theme options like light and dark mode to match your preference.

How fast does the player load songs?

The player is optimized for speed, so songs load almost instantly depending on your internet connection and file size.

Will there be updates in the future?

Yes, the player will continue to receive improvements, new features, and performance updates over time.
